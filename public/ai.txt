# Monster Project Group (MPG)

> Monster Project Group is a luxury construction project management and owner's representation firm in South Florida. MPG protects the project owner's budget, schedule, and contract — coordinating contractors, architects, and engineers from preconstruction through closeout. Service area: Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties.
